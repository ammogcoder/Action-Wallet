Updated to Action Coin 1.11.10.

The Action Coin Platform represents over 1 Million Action collectors and customers 
from more than 200 countries and territories. It is the basis of an 
incentive-driven rewards program with “points” that are rewarded in the form of a
decentralized, blockchain-based digital asset known as Action (“ACTN”). Action may
be redeemed for member-only discounts on a growing list of products and services 
offered through our business network.
